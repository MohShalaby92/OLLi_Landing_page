About OLLi

Our Inspiration

OLLi was born from a frustrating online shopping experience. After spending hours searching for product information across multiple pages, we wondered: "What if you could just ask questions and get immediate answers?" This experience inspired us to create an AI assistant that makes online shopping as natural as talking to a knowledgeable store clerk.

This project was developed as a Portfolio Project for ALX Africa, completed in four weeks from concept to MVP.
